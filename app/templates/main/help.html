Help & Documentation

Getting Started

  1. Register an Account

    Create your account and wait for admin approval.

  2. Connect Microsoft 365

    Go to Settings and connect your Microsoft account to access real emails and calendar.

  3. Generate Your Digest

    Click "How's My Day?" button to generate your AI-powered daily digest.

Features

4D Email Classification

Your emails are automatically categorized using the 4D framework:

  • Do: Tasks requiring immediate action
  • Delegate: Tasks to assign to others
  • Defer: Tasks to schedule for later
  • Delete: Informational emails requiring no action
Calendar Analysis

Get insights about your daily schedule including:

  • Meeting summaries with attendees and agendas
  • Total meeting hours
  • Available focus time between meetings
Privacy Mode

Enable privacy mode in settings to automatically redact personal information from your digests.

FAQ

Why can I only generate one digest per day?

To encourage healthy email habits and prevent information overload.

What permissions does the app need?

The app requires read-only access to your emails and calendar. We never modify or delete your data.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and never store your email content permanently.
